# SMP & FAIR - best practices for research software

This document aims to provide guidance in writing a Software Management Plan (SMP) for a FAIR research software project along with best practices for software development and management.
By writing an SMP and adopting these best practices, you will save time, increase the value of your software, and make it easier for others to use and build upon your work.

This guide follows the natural workflow of creating a research software project, with a focus on embedding sustainability and FAIR practices at every stage.
